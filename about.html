WELCOME TO ARIGATO
SUSHI RESTAURANT


Arigato serves traditional and innovative Japanese cuisine. The atmosphere is casual,
while a modern and upscale design contribute to a relaxing and enjoyable dining
experience. An eclectic sushi bar is paired with a full-scale Japanese kitchen offering
traditional Japanese dishes as well as a full liquor bar featuring ten kinds of sake.


In its 15 years long history, the Arigato restaurant has grown to become one of the
biggest Japanese dining destinations located in New York Together as a bug team of
cooking pros we’ve introduced Americans to a wide variety of new and classic Japanese
meals, strengthening the cultural exchange between our two great nations.


We invite you to join us and experience for yourself one of the finest
Japanese restaurants and sushi bars.
